# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Individual professionals, founders, and client-facing operators who manage work through Gmail and Google Calendar.

## Product Purpose

Autobot gives users one secure command center for reviewing email, calendar events, and integration health. Success means users understand what needs attention and can act without switching between disconnected tools.

## Brand Personality

Calm, capable, and quietly playful.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic startup landing pages, dense enterprise dashboards, childish mascots, decorative AI gradients, and interfaces that make security or permissions unclear.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the user's day, not the underlying integrations.
- Keep actions understandable, close to their context, and reversible.
- Use warmth to reduce friction without reducing clarity.
- Show the product through specific stories and useful previews.
- Reveal complexity only when the user needs it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and interaction requirements. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus states, readable body text, and reduced-motion preferences.
